美国计算机硕士不接受雅思,美国留学：不接受雅思的大学盘点

我们知道，其实越来越多的美国大学都接受雅思申请了，但也还有部分是不接受的，下面以美国会计硕士为例，看看目前还有哪些美国大学不接受雅思。

往年不接受而今年开始接受的项目比如：

Southern Methodist University：Master of Science in Accounting

New York University：MS in Accounting

Wake Forest University：Master of Science in Accountancy

The Admissions Committee recommends a minimum TOEFL score of 110 (IBT) or an official IELTS score of 8.0.

目前还不接受雅思的项目如：

University of Michigan-Ann Arbor：Master of Accounting

We do not accept IELTS scores.

University of North Carolina-Chapel Hill：Master of Accounting

(学校邮件回复)

Lehigh University：

MS Accounting and Information Analysis

International students must provide Test of English as a Foreign Language score [TOEFL] (NOTE: IELTS not accepted)

University of Florida：Master of Accounting

IELTS scores are not accepted as a replacement for the TOEFL requirement.

Indiana University—Bloomington：Master of Science in Accounting

Any applicant who is a non-citizen will require a TOEFL, even if you have a US degree. We do not accept IELTS scores.

New York University：Master's Program in Media, Culture, and Communication

NYU Steinhardt does not accept scores from other equivalent exams, including IELTS.

New York University：MA in International Relations

Can I submit IELTS scores instead of TOEFL scores? No.

Northwestern University：Master of Science in Communication

The MSC Program does not accept the IELTS test.

Michigan State University：Master of Arts in Public Relations

The department does not accept the IELTS in place of the TOEFL.

学校每一年的要求也会有变动，因此，在即将申请之前，大家一定要留意或者向学校确认清楚哦~
