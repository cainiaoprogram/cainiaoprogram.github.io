二，语法（知识点归纳）

 一，情态动词

 1.can

表示能力

        We can speak a little English.

        Her sister can play the violin.

        I can play badminton, but I can't play volleyball.

表示许可

        You can play basketball on weekends.

        We can't run in the hallways.

表示请求                   

        Can you help me with my English ?

2.may

表示许可

        May I borrow your dictionary?

        Class is over.You may go now.

表示可能性(may be)

        I  may be late ,so don't wait for me.

        That may be true.

例外：表示月份（5月）

        It's May.
