dataframe 怎么提取指定的某几行

代码如下：
import pandas as pd  # 将pandas作为第三方库导入，我们一般为pandas取一个别名叫做pd
dfzz=pd.DataFrame({"a":[1.78,1.8,2.8,2.75,5,5,23],"b":[20.8,10,10,30,43,1,12],"c":[23,15,50,3,343,12,95]})
am=pd.DataFrame({"a":[None],"b":[None]})
print(dfzz)
print(am)
print("")

data2 = dfzz.loc[2:3]
print(data2)
