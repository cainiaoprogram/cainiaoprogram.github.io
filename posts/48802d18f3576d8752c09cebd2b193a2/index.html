题目描述

X 市建了一个新的体育馆，每日人流量信息被记录在这三列信息中：序号 (id)、日期 (visit_date)、 人流量 (people)。

请编写一个查询语句，找出人流量的高峰期。高峰期时，至少连续三行记录中的人流量不少于100。


题解

SELECT A.*
FROM
    stadium A
INNER JOIN
    stadium B
ON A.id=B.id+1
INNER JOIN
    stadium C
ON A.id=C.id+2
WHERE A.people>=100
    AND B.people>=100
    AND C.people>=100
UNION
SELECT B.*
FROM
    stadium A
INNER JOIN
    stadium B
ON A.id=B.id+1
INNER JOIN
    stadium C
ON A.id=C.id+2
WHERE A.people>=100
    AND B.people>=100
    AND C.people>=100
UNION
SELECT C.*
FROM
    stadium A
INNER JOIN
    stadium B
ON A.id=B.id+1
INNER JOIN
    stadium C
ON A.id=C.id+2
WHERE A.people>=100
    AND B.people>=100
    AND C.people>=100
ORDER BY id;
